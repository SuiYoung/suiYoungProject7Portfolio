Hi! I'm Sui,

Pronounced "Soo-ey", this is a part of my name written in Jiaguwen, or Oracle Bone Script, the earliest form of Chinese writing. Often used in divination for the royal Shang court, and the name I choose to go by! "Sui" means lucky, auspicious, and in general a good omen. It also sounds similar to the word for water and serenity in Cantonese!
